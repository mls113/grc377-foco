Have your coffee with a cat!

Cat Beans Cafe is a small locally owned business where guests can come for a coffee or tea and relax with rescue cats and kittens. Located in downtown San Luis Obispo and partnered with the local cat shelter, the cafe is home to the kittens and cats in need of socialization before being put up for adoption.


Did you know that 15% of profits & 100% of Cat Beans Cafes' tips are donated to the Cal Poly Cat Program?
